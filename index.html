Mapping USAID’s Public-Private Partnerships in Global Health

USAID’s database of public-private partnerships shows that the agency has formed more than 3,500 public-private partnerships since 2001. In the global health and water sanitation sectors, USAID reports that it has worked with more than 1,200 partners on over 330 projects in 76 countries, leveraging more than $7.5 billion.

Explore the map to learn more about these partnerships, which US Fortune 500 companies are involved, and how public and private funding has been distributed to global health programs across the globe.
